Why does Google flag this?

The Logic: To automate your work, this script needs permission to act on your behalf, specifically to send emails (Gmail) and save PDFs (Drive).

The Reason: Google treats any script that sends emails as "Unverified" unless it has gone through a formal corporate security audit (which takes weeks). Since this is a free, open-source portfolio project, it hasn't undergone that corporate process.

Your Safety: The script runs entirely inside your Google account. No data is ever sent to me or any external server. You are the owner of the code the moment you copy the sheet.
